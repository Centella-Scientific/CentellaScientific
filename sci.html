• Journal selection & submission

Improve your chances of publication by letting Centella help you match with the most suitable journals for your research article. Upon sharing your criteria, our team will suggest you with top 3-5 journals that fit in the area of your work.

Criteria for selecting the journal :

1.Target journal type

2. Indexing(PubMed, Scopus, SCI, Web of Science..etc)

3. Impact factor

4. Type of article

• Rejected/Revised paper editing

Centella offers customized editing services in the fixing the rejected manuscript either by resubmitting to the same target journal with substantive or communicating to new journal after enhancing the quality of the manuscript. Our subject matter experts ensure that your manuscript revised and rewritten meticulously as per the reviewer comments and drafts a strong rebuttal to the queries.

• Artwork preparation

Centella’s experienced artwork editors, armed with latest software tools will check and address all the technical aspects of your manuscript such as color, resolution (DPI or PPI), file size to confirm adherence to journal guidelines.

• Video abstract

“Stand out from the crowd”- With more than 5000 manuscripts published every day, it will be challenging task to make your work stand out. Off late Videos have emerged as an important medium to reach out and connect with a wider target audience. With the help of Centella’s video abstract service, you can transform your published manuscript into a top quality engaging and impactful video that recapitulates and showcase the essential findings. Our team of creative experts with scientific background will create 2-3 minute audiovisual file that you can share, post in social media handles and even present at symposiums that enhance your chance of funding and citations.
