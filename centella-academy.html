CENTELLA ACADEMY

The objective of Centella Academy is to build the scientific rigor and inculcate research temperament at gross root level in school children. Children given opportunity to design and develop scientific methodology for a research problem statement and will be mentored by a pool of subject matter experts. This exercise introduces the scientific research component and get tangible outcomes in the form of publication/ patent/ product.
