ABOUT CENTELLA

Centella was founded by Dr.Riyaz Syed to help researchers to turn their scientific ideas into high impact publications in order to reach out to the global audience and enable their work to create impact. While as a Ph.D research scholar, RS used to mentor and guide his junior research colleagues in drafting the manuscripts, dissertations, thesis. During his 4 yrs of Post-doctoral tenure his unwavering passion for scientific writing has further intensified and made him to support his fellow colleagues in writing & winning successful national and international grant proposals. Post his tenure as a CSIR-Pool Scientist, he founded Centella with a purpose to help Non-native English-speaking researcher’s scientific discoveries turn into solutions. Centella is founded and lead by experienced & practicing research scientists. The team has a 60+yrs of cumulative academic and industrial research experiences in organic medicinal chemistry, cancer biology, molecular biology, microbiology, biotechnology, drug discovery, and chemi/bio informatics.
