MEDICAL COMMUNICATIONS

Accurate, clearly written, scientifically sound and well-articulated documents are key to timely approval of drugs that get submitted for regulatory approval. Centella supports all the scientific and medical communication needs of Pharma, Bio-tech and Medical industries. Our comprehensive mix of clinicians, doctors, writers and scientists from life sciences sector with MD, PhD, MBBS, and Masters degrees has expertise to provide solutions to all your medical writing needs. Centella has thorough discernment of various regulatory guidelines of the international bodies such as FDA, GCP, ICH, and EMA. Our team of medical writers help you to articulate and write documents that are required throughout the drug development process. We adopt stringent quality check protocols and seamless processes to address needs of diverse stakeholders including patients, Institutional Review Boards (IRB), clinical investigators, study sponsors, and medical coordinators.
